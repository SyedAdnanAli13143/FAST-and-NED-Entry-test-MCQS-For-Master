# "Some Questions Seemed Old" — Why, and Where to Find Them

## Why questions feel recycled

FAST's own bank (and most Pakistani university entry tests) overlaps heavily with the **NTS GAT-General** question style and with question pools that circulate across dozens of prep websites and Facebook/WhatsApp study groups. The same 200–500 "classic" analytical/quantitative questions get reused, slightly reworded, year after year across many universities. That's exactly why a question can feel familiar even on a first attempt — you may have seen a near-identical version while scrolling a prep group, or it's a standard textbook-style item.

**Practical implication:** practicing from *any* solid GAT-General / analytical-reasoning question bank has real transfer value to the FAST test, not just FAST-branded material.

## Official source (start here)

- **FAST Test Pattern (official):** https://www.nu.edu.pk/Admissions/TestPattern — confirms the weightages used in file `00`.
- **NTS GAT-General official curriculum:** https://nts.org.pk/products/gatgen/gat-g-curriculum.php — the closest "official" description of what Analytical/Quantitative/Verbal reasoning actually covers nationally.
