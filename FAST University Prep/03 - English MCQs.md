## C. Sentence Correction

**Q1.** Choose the grammatically correct sentence:
A) He don't like coffee.  B) He doesn't likes coffee.  C) He doesn't like coffee.  D) He not like coffee.
**Answer: C)**

**Q2.** Choose the grammatically correct sentence:
A) Neither of the boys were present.  B) Neither of the boys was present.  C) Neither of the boy was present.  D) Neither of the boys is being present.
**Answer: B)** — "Neither" takes a singular verb.

**Q3.** Choose the grammatically correct sentence:
A) She has went to market.  B) She has gone to market.  C) She have gone to market.  D) She had go to market.
**Answer: B)**

**Q4.** Choose the grammatically correct sentence:
A) I have been living here since five years.  B) I have been living here for five years.  C) I am living here since five years.  D) I live here since five years.
**Answer: B)** — use "for" with a duration, "since" with a starting point (e.g. "since 2020").

**Q5.** Choose the grammatically correct sentence:
A) Each of the students have submitted their assignment.  B) Each of the students has submitted his assignment.  C) Each of the student has submitted their assignment.  D) Each of the students have submit assignment.
**Answer: B)** — "Each" takes a singular verb.

## F. Short Reading Comprehension (practice the skill, not just vocabulary)

> Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the ability of machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions. While early AI systems followed fixed rules written by programmers, modern AI systems learn patterns directly from large amounts of data. This shift, often called machine learning, has made AI useful in fields ranging from medicine to finance.

**Q1.** According to the passage, how do modern AI systems mainly learn?
A) By following fixed rules  B) By learning patterns from data  C) By copying human behaviour directly  D) By using calculators
**Answer: B)**

**Q2.** The word "fields" in the passage most likely means:
A) Agricultural land  B) Areas of activity/application  C) Sports grounds  D) Empty spaces
**Answer: B)**

**Reading comprehension strategy:** read the question stems first, then skim the passage hunting for those specific words/ideas rather than reading every line carefully — this is faster under time pressure.
